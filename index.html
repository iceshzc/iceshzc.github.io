Zhichao Huang

PhD candidate

Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen

I am a final-year PhD student in Machine Learning at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, advised by Prof. Yunming Ye. And my chinese name is 黃智超. My main area of interest is learning with graph deep learning and structured representations/computations. This includes topics in reasoning, recommendation, and knowledge graph applications.

My formal background is in Computer Science and Technology (M.Sc. hons. 2016, B.Sc. 2013 at HIT and NCU, respectively). During my studies, I have had exposure to a number of fields and—after a short interlude in random forest, spectral learning, eventually found my way into graph deep learning.
