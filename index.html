Zhichao Huang

DMT

JD.com, Inc.

My name is Zhichao Huang (黃智超) and now work as an Algorithm Engineer with JD iCity. Before that, I spent half a year as a Doctor Management Trainee (DMT) with JD.com, Inc. Previously, I was a Ph.D. student in Data Mining at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, advised by Prof. Yunming Ye. My main area of interest is learning with graph deep learning and structured representations/computations. This includes topics in reasoning, recommendation, and knowledge graph applications.

My formal background are in Computer Software and Theory (Ph.D. 2021 at HIT) and in Computer Science and Technology (M.Sc. 2016, B.Sc. 2013 at HIT and NCU, respectively). During my studies, I have had exposure to a number of fields and—after a short interlude in random forest, spectral learning, eventually found my way into graph deep learning.
