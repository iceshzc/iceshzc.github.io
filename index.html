Zhichao Huang

Algorithmic Engineer

JD iCity

My name is Zhichao Huang (黃智超), and I have earned the prestigious "Yi Qilin" Excellent Talent award from the Beijing Economic-Technological Development Area, as well as the recognition of Associate Fellow by the Beijing Natural Science Research Series Committee. Currently, I am serving as an Algorithmic Engineer at JD iCity. Before that, I spent half a year as a Doctor Management Trainee (DMT) at JD.com, Inc. Previously, I was a Ph.D. student in Data Mining at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, advised by Prof. Yunming Ye.

My primary field of interest centers on graph deep learning and the development of structured representations and computations. This encompasses subjects such as reasoning, recommendation systems, and knowledge graph analytics, among others. During my studies, I have had exposure to a number of fields and—after a short interlude in random forest, spectral learning, eventually found my way into graph deep learning.
